Pill-Up! is the best resolution to a complicated problem, when to take what medicine. If you ever find yourself, or your hospital have struggle with notifying yourself or the patients to take a certain pill. This is the way to go!

It manages each patient's prescription and then the new information will be updated to the server. It will also notifies the patients when to check and pick up their medication.
